Every day get 3 time points:
    - Morning 6-11 (5+d6)
    - Noon 11-16 (10+d6)
    - Evening 16-21 (15+d6)
    - Night 21-6 (20+d6)

The players can choose what they spend the time points on. ex:
- Traveling
- Investigating a Location
- Stopping at a Settlement
- Finding A Water Source
- Hunting Prey
